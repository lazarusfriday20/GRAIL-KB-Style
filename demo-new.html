Admonitions

Caution

Caution means that there is possible damage to equipment or user data.

Important

Use an Important admonition to show the user a piece of information that should not be overlooked. While this information may not change anything the user is doing, it should show the user that this piece of information could be vital.

Note

A note is a piece of supplemental information that is not crucial, though it may be useful. Use to help users deal with a potential issue that may occur in the process.
